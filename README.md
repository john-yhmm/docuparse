# DocuParse

**Drowning in invoices? DocuParse pulls you out — instantly.**

Upload any invoice or receipt. AI pulls out everything you need in seconds. No more manual typing. No more lost documents. No more wasted hours.

---

## What You Get

- **Instant Extraction** — Vendor, date, total, tax, line items. Done in seconds, not minutes.
- **Everything in One Place** — Every document saved, searchable, and ready when you need it.
- **See Where Your Money Goes** — A live dashboard that breaks down spending by vendor, category, and month.
- **Export in One Click** — CSV export ready for Excel, QuickBooks, or your accountant.
- **You Stay in Control** — AI does the heavy lifting. You approve, correct, or edit with one click.

---

## Who It's For

- Small business owners drowning in receipts
- Accountants handling high invoice volumes
- Freelancers tracking business expenses
- Procurement teams tired of manual data entry

---

## How It Works

1. **Upload** — Any invoice, any format, any layout
2. **AI Extracts** — Structured data appears instantly
3. **Review** — Correct anything in seconds
4. **Done** — Search, analyze, and export anytime

---

## Built With

React · Node.js · Express · PostgreSQL · Claude AI API
